The Problem

The issue we encountered stemmed from the implementation of new security measures due to Covid, which necessitated additional time for us to access the computer and unlock the door.

The Solution:

Our implementation of the door unlock system has alleviated time and fatigue for individuals who require frequent access to the door. The system has also helped our efficiency by providing a more time-effective method of access.

Our development of the door unlocking mechanism via remote has successfully saved users time when opening the door for people entering the office. This solution has not only reduced the strain on individuals who need to frequently access the door, but also improved our overall efficiency by offering a more time-efficient means of entry.
